Description

This is one of the original ~50 cars that Motorex imported with copies of the DOT bond release included. Being one of the rare Motorex cars the speedometer is in MPG and it has other compliance modifications like BMW side markers. I have owned it for almost 12 years and kept it in a garage or in an underground parking garage when I used it as a daily. I was told it had some front end damage and the hood, fenders, and radiator support were replaced by a Nissan dealer with Nissan parts. The car was completely stripped and then painted in the Le Mans Sunset Metallic in 2007. Due to this it has Rebuilt title history and the price is set accordingly. All the soft interior was redone in suede and does show its age. Plastics, dash are all in great shape. Car was originally white and the front bumper does have rock chips from regular driving and a small door ding in the left rear fender.
